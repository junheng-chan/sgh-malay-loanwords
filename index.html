Listen to selected tokens while viewing the corresponding spectrograms and annotations.

Chan Junheng · Nanyang Technological University · Preliminary dataset

Scope and data use

These are selected illustrative tokens from a preliminary dataset. Speaker labels are anonymised. Please do not download, redistribute, or reuse the audio without permission. If consent does not allow public audio sharing, replace clips with spectrograms only or make the repository private.

How to use this page

Each card pairs a short audio clip with a spectrogram image, source form, Singaporean Hokkien realisation, and a brief annotation. Use the filters to focus on direct, adaptive, variable, or uncertain correspondences.

Legend

directclose Malay–SgH match
adaptivemapped to nearest SgH category
variabledifferent outputs across tokens/speakers
uncertainrequires further checking
